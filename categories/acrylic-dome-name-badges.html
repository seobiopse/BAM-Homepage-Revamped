Why use acrylic name badges in Australia?

Acrylic is an ideal material for supporting clear lettering and your brand’s designs. Acrylic doming also provides a sense of prestige, making them ideal for almost any setting.

On a practical level, using custom magnetic name badges that are made from acrylic is hygienic. They’re easy to wipe down, making them ideal for healthcare settings, restaurants, and other such environments.

While remaining entirely cost-effective, these custom badges support bright colours. As such, if you’re looking for magnetic name badges in Melbourne that won’t blow through your budget, acrylic is the way forward.

Acrylic name badges that are durable

In addition to looking professional, acrylic name badges are durable. They stand the test of time without losing their shine, making acrylic your go-to material when you want magnetic name badges that’ll go the extra mile.

Using acrylic for your magnetic name badges in Australia also allows you to achieve a shape that matches your brand. If you prefer smooth-round edges over sharp rectangular designs, we can make it happen.
